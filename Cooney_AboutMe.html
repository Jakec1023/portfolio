Jake Cooney's Online Portfolio

I feel that, now in 2018 if you want to succeed in this world you must attend at least some form of education after high school. I knew that I would be going to college since the fifth grade seeing as how that was when the word seemed to start being heard when I was around my parents and relatives more often. They would always tell me to "Study hard, so you can go to college Jake!" I would always just reply with indifference, not fully understanding the gravity of having to go to school for another four years. However, now I understand. I realize now that if I want to make money, have a job, and someday start a family, I must go to college. So for now, in my life, that is my goal. Get into a good college. This means I have to work hard and get good grades. I have to do this if I want to one day get a job, make money, and be comfortable.

My name is Jake Edward Cooney and I am a high school student living with my family in Dublin California. Seeing as this is my first year in high school I still have a long way to go until I do achieve my goal of getting into a good college but I like to think ahead. I know that it takes many things for a good college to accept you, and I do have a few achievements already under my belt. First I have good grades so far into my high school carreer sitting with mostly A's and a few B's. However as many teachers told me that middle school grades "do not really matter", I was not persuaded by them. I achieved a 4.0 GPA in nearly every quarter of my three years in middle school.

I may be a smart student however I would say that I am also an acceptable leader when it comes to sports. Over my short fifteen years on this Earth I have competed for many sports teams. My main sports so far have been football and baseball. I previously lived on the East Coast in Delaware before my family moved to California. When I moved to California I discovered how truly competitive baseball really is. After a few years of getting acclimated to the better competition in the state of California I joined a travel baseball team called the Thundersox. With my second year on the team I became the captain and lead our team to numerous championship medals throughout the next two years. Then, after our team had finally disbanded I was entering high school. This is when I started my football career. I met even more new people and made great friends with many of them. However all great things do come to an end and the season ended, our team with a record of 7 wins and 3 losses. In the final awards ceremony I was awarded the MVP award for the season and that was the end of it. I hope to make the baseball team in the spring and work hard for football next season so our record can be even better. Thank you for finding out a little bit about me and feel free to explore the rest of my portfolio.

This winter/spring I started my first season of highschool baseball. I was coming off of a broken finger from football and I wasn't really confident in my abilities that I was used to using. I am a pretty good baseball player or at least I was before highschool, but I wasn't truly ready. I started the season off on a bad note. Before I knew it my ERA was in teh double digits and I was hitting in the 7 spot. My entire life I had been hitting in the top 5 of the lineup so I was shocked. However this was warranted. I wasn't playing well and it was showing. So I started putting in more hours with other coaches and just hitting in my garage. I started throwing more and for longer. I was getting my velocity that I used to have back. I could play baseball again. Now nearing the end of the season my team has a positive record and we are facing our rivals Dougherty Valley. If we win this game we will be solidified as one of the best teams in our league. Lets see how that goes.
