Scratch Project

Completed:September 7th

In my Computer Science Principles class one of our first projects had to do with the Scratch programming language. We started with this language because it is generally considered a pretty basic language. I worked with a partner and even though we were pretty new to programming. However I feel that we both did our part to complete the project. We worked together and decided to come up with a mix between Super Mario Bros. and Super Smash Bros. We worked on the layout of the game while in class and while at home. However the homework was more one sided because I didn't have access to a shared game file from my home computer.
