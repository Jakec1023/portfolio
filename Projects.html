Scratch Project

In my Computer Scicence Principles class one of our first projects had to do with the Scratch programming language. We started with this language because it is generally considered a pretty basic language. I worked with a partner and even though I am pretty new to programming I feel that we both did our part to complete the project. We worked together and decided to come up with a sort of mix between Super Mario Bros. and Super Smash Bros. We worked on the Layout of the game while in class and while at home. However the home work was more one sided because I didn't have access to a shared game file from my home computer.
